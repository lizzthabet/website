ABOUT

Lizz Thabet is a multimedia artist and developer from Raleigh, North Carolina. She holds a BFA in photography and visual culture from Rochester Institute of Technology, and works in digital publishing. Through zines, drawings, and wearables, Lizz tries to make living pictures that will speak back to her. She lives in Brooklyn, NY.

Ask her about zines, collaborating, rainbow sprinkles, etc, etc.
