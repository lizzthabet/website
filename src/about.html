ABOUT

Lizz Thabet is an artist, powerlifter, and programmer who loves digital storytelling. She holds a BFA in photography and visual culture from Rochester Institute of Technology and graduated from the Web Development Fellowship at Fullstack Academy of Code. Her work pulls together drawings, code, and wearables to play with "living pictures" and alternative masculinities. In 2017, Lizz participated in Triple Canopy’s Publication Intensive and joined Women’s Studio Workshop as a board member. She currently works at Attune as a software engineer.
