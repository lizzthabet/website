I AM HIDING ALL YEAR ROUND

An unfinished collection of experiments for disappearing my body in public space

Placeholder text: I am hiding all year round flirts with my fantasies of hiding, disappearance, and misrecognition. The collection includes a homemade camouflage pattern generator and a set of full-body suits (with patterns for each season) based on my neighborhood.

Placeholder text: Camouflage is a visual language and technology of concealment--to hide and hide that you are hiding. I use camouflage in part to escape "femaleness," as Andrea Long Chu frames it--the universal and existential condition of being emptied out for another's desires--and in part to connect more deeply to the joy of being seen. To conceal is also to reveal.
