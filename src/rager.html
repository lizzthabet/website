DONE FEELIN MY FEELINGS (2021) is a small collection of drawings about emotional labor, femme anger, and the end of the world. The book follows two personas, one self-destructive and one vengeful, as they meld into one another and refuse the work of feeling.

Each book comes with a postcard invitation to draw: What does your anger look like? Does it have a body?
