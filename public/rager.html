DONE FEELIN MY FEELINGS

Welcome to the DONE FEELIN rager

A virtual book launch,
shared online album,
not-another-Zoom-party party
that happened on June 30, 2021

DONE FEELIN MY FEELINGS (2021) is a small collection of drawings about emotional labor, femme anger, and an end of the world. The book follows two personas, one self-destructive and one vengeful, as they meld into one another and refuse the work of feeling.

Each book comes with a postcard invitation to draw: What does your anger look like? Does it have a body?

People contributed to the collaborative group photo album during the launch.

How do I buy a book?

Books are available starting at $4, plus $7 flat rate for shipping. $4 covers the cost of book production and any amount paid above that (not including shipping) will be donated to Bed-Stuy Strong, a mutual aid network in Brooklyn, NY.

If you'd like to purchase a book, you can email me (lizzthabet@proton), Venmo me (@lizzthabet), or Paypal me (paypal.me/lizzthabet). Please include your mailing address and how many copies you'd like if ordering more than one. If you want to do a pickup in Brooklyn, that's an option, too!

Books will ship within a week of purchase.

Where does my photo data live?

Any images shared through the upload form were stored as a base64 encoded string in a Firebase database managed by me and later downloaded for this site. No personal or device information was collected by me on image upload. Images will not used outside of the context of this project. If you have any concerns or questions, please reach out.
