DIES ALONE

A collaboration with Evan Ortiz.

A home-made meme and collection generated from text exchanges between two friends that describe normal life events with the punchline dies alone. On April 1st, 2017, the first collection of zines, blankets, hats, scarves, and balloons launched with a pop-up shop in Brooklyn, NY.
